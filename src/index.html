Pandemic Tutorial

Thank you so much for choosing to play our own take on the pandemic game. Now before you play, you might want to read this tutorial

To begin, Pandemic is a strategy game where you are trying to rid the world of a deadly outbreak of a disease. You can travel throughout the world and conduct research on a cure for the disease. Once you develop a cure, you win the game. If you fail to develop a cure and the disease grows too much. You will lose the game.

Rules and Gameplay:

  • Player begins with 4 action points to spend. The player can spend these points by travelling to a differenct city, treating the disease, or conducting research.

  • To win the game, you must develop a cure and eradicate the disease.

  • If the infected population maxes out, you will lose the game.

  • When the game starts, 3 cities in the world will be automatically infected.

  • You have the option to skip your turn and restore your action points.
